Lasagna

Pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce. Lasagna is a first course consisting of layers of thin pasta dough made with flour, egg and spinach. Between each layer are ragù sauce and béchamel. The top layer is lightly sprinkled with Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, the preparation is then baked.

Adana Kebab

A Turkish dish consisting of seasoned minced lamb, which is prepared on a skewer over a charcoal fire. This recipe seasons the lamb with onion, garlic, cumin, sumac, and red pepper flakes. The cooked kebabs are often served over warm flatbread to catch the drippings and are accompanied by roasted tomatoes, green or red peppers, sliced onions, and parsley.
